What is this?

Broadway shows raffle cheap tickets every day — this bot enters for you

Most Broadway shows offer a daily digital lottery: a limited number of tickets at a heavily discounted price (usually $35–$49, compared to $150–$400 face value) are raffled off to anyone who enters their name. The lottery opens the day before each performance and closes a few hours later. Winners are notified by email and have about 60 minutes to pay.

The catch: you have to enter every single day, for every show you want to see, across multiple websites (Broadway Direct, Lucky Seat, Telecharge). Each entry takes a few minutes of form-filling. It adds up fast if you're trying to see several shows.

This script automates that entire process. You set up your info once, select which shows you want to enter, and click one button. The bot opens every lottery page, fills every form, and submits your entries — all in under a minute. You just need to solve a reCAPTCHA when one appears.

Getting started

Installation in 5 steps

⚠ Before you start — create your accounts

Some platforms require you to be logged in for the bot to work. Create these free accounts before installing:

1

Install Tampermonkey

Go to tampermonkey.net and click Download for your browser (Chrome, Firefox, or Edge). A popup will ask you to confirm — click Add extension. It's completely free.

2

Allow Tampermonkey to run scripts — Chrome only

This one-time step is required on Chrome. Right-click the Tampermonkey icon in the top-right corner of your browser → click Manage extension. On that page, find the toggle Allow User Scripts and turn it on.

3

Download and install the script

Click the ⬇ Download button at the top of this page. A new tab will open — Tampermonkey detects the script automatically and shows an Install button. Click it to confirm. Done.

4

Fill in your personal data

Go to bwayrush.com. A gold 🎭 button appears in the bottom-right corner. Click it to open the panel, then click My Data to expand the settings. Fill in your details — this is done only once, your data is saved automatically in your browser.

5

Select shows and launch

Click on the shows you want to enter — they highlight in gold when selected. Choose ◎ Manual or ⚡ Auto mode (explained below), then click 🚀 Open lotteries. Each lottery opens in a new tab and is handled automatically.

Auto vs Manual

Choosing your mode

The ◎ Manual / ⚡ Auto toggle in the filter bar applies to all selected shows at once. Here's what each mode does:

◎ Manual

Opens and fills — you click Enter

The bot opens each lottery page in a new tab and fills in all the form fields (name, email, date of birth, zip, tickets). It stops there and waits. You review the form, solve the reCAPTCHA if one appears, and click Enter yourself. Use this mode when you want to double-check each entry before submitting, or when a show has specific requirements you want to review.

⚡ Auto

Opens, fills, and submits automatically

The bot opens each lottery page, fills the entire form, and clicks Enter without any input from you. If a reCAPTCHA appears, it pauses and asks you to solve it — then continues automatically. Use this mode for your daily routine when you trust the settings and want to enter as many lotteries as possible with minimal effort. After launching, you only need to solve captchas when they appear.

How it works

What the bot does on each platform

Broadway Direct Auto

  • Runs on lottery.broadwaydirect.com when you open a show page
  • Finds all "ENTER NOW" buttons and processes them one by one
  • Fills name, email, date of birth, zip, country and tickets automatically
  • In Auto mode: clicks Enter if no captcha is present
  • In Manual mode: fills the form and waits for you to click Enter
  • After each submission, detects the success screen and moves to the next show
  • If no lotteries are open, shows a message explaining why

Lucky Seat Auto

  • Runs on any luckyseat.com/shows/... page when you open it
  • Selects performances based on your Showtimes filter setting
  • Weekend shows (Sat/Sun) are always fully selected regardless of filter
  • Skips performances already entered (shown in red — disabled buttons)
  • Shows a message if all performances for your filter are already entered
  • In Auto mode: clicks Submit Entry if no captcha is present
  • In Manual mode: selects performances and sets tickets, waits for you to submit

Telecharge Auto

  • Runs on my.socialtoaster.com — all shows appear on a single page
  • Reads which show you selected in the panel and only enters that one
  • Sets the ticket count to match your preference
  • In Auto mode: clicks Enter for that show automatically
  • In Manual mode: sets tickets and highlights the correct show — you click Enter
  • Shows a message if that show is not listed or already entered
  • Requires being logged in at my.socialtoaster.com

FAQ

Questions & answers

Is this script safe to use? Will I get banned? ›
The script runs entirely in your browser and stores no data externally. It mimics normal human behavior — filling forms and clicking buttons. That said, lottery terms of service prohibit automated entries. Use at your own discretion.
Why does it sometimes ask me to solve a reCAPTCHA? ›
reCAPTCHA is a challenge designed to detect bots and requires human interaction. It cannot be bypassed automatically. When detected, the script fills everything else and waits for you to click "I'm not a robot." Many sessions have no captcha at all — in those cases the script submits fully automatically.
What's the difference between Auto and Manual mode? ›
In Manual mode the bot opens each page and fills the form, but you click Enter yourself. In Auto mode the bot fills the form and clicks Enter automatically — you only need to intervene if a reCAPTCHA appears. You switch between them using the ◎ Manual / ⚡ Auto toggle in the panel before launching.
Broadway Direct shows no "ENTER NOW" buttons — what happened? ›
A banner appears after a few seconds explaining the possible reason: all lotteries may be Closed or Upcoming, you may have already entered all available performances, or the lottery window hasn't opened yet. Check the schedule shown on the show page.
Lucky Seat says "already entered all performances" — what does that mean? ›
All performances that match your Showtimes filter (mornings/evenings) are already entered — they appear as red disabled buttons on the page. There's nothing left to do for this show today. Check your email for results, or try a different show.
Can I enter the same show twice? ›
On Broadway Direct, submitting duplicate entries for the same performance results in disqualification. On Lucky Seat, your latest entry automatically replaces the previous one — re-entering is completely safe. On Telecharge, the bot detects if you've already entered and skips the show.
I won! What do I do next? ›
Check your email immediately — you have about 60 minutes to claim and pay for your tickets via credit card. On Broadway Direct you can also check your status at lottery.broadwaydirect.com. On Lucky Seat, log into your account dashboard. Missing the payment window means your tickets are released to another winner.
Why are TodayTix and Hamilton not supported? ›
TodayTix requires a native mobile app — their lottery is not available on the web. Hamilton's lottery is managed through its own dedicated platform with strong anti-bot protections. Both are intentionally excluded from this script.
