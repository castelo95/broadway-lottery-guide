Getting started

Installation in 4 steps

1

Install Tampermonkey

Download Tampermonkey for your browser — Chrome, Firefox or Edge. It's free and takes 30 seconds to install.

2

Download the script

Click the Download button above. Tampermonkey will detect it automatically and open an installation page. Click Install to confirm.

3

Fill in your data

Go to bwayrush.com. A gold 🎭 button appears bottom-right. Click it, open My Data, and fill in your name, email, date of birth and zip code.

4

Select shows and go

Check the shows you want to enter and click 🚀 Open lotteries. Each lottery page opens in a new tab and fills itself automatically.

How it works

Platform behaviour

Broadway Direct Auto

  • Opens each "ENTER NOW" lottery in sequence
  • Auto-fills name, email, DOB, zip, country, tickets
  • No reCAPTCHA → clicks ENTER automatically
  • reCAPTCHA → waits for you, then continues
  • Detects SUCCESS modal, closes it, moves to next show
  • Shows a clear message if no open lotteries found

Lucky Seat Auto

  • Runs on any luckyseat.com/shows/... page
  • Selects performances by your time-of-day filter
  • Weekends (Sat/Sun) always get all showtimes
  • Skips already-entered slots (shown in red)
  • Tells you if all your filter's slots are entered
  • Auto-submits when no captcha is present

Best practices

Tips for more wins

🎯
Enter every day. Lotteries reset per performance. Consistency dramatically increases your odds over time.
📬
Watch your email. You usually have only 60 minutes to claim your tickets after winning. Don't miss the window.
🌙
Try matinées. Evening shows have more competition. Morning/afternoon lotteries tend to have better odds.
🎭
Enter multiple shows. You can win tickets to more than one show for the same night — enter as many as you want.
🪪
Use your real name. Winners must show photo ID at the box office. Nicknames get your tickets cancelled.
🔄
Lucky Seat re-entry is safe. Your latest entry automatically replaces the previous one — no risk of disqualification.
FAQ

Questions & answers

Is this script safe? Will I get banned? ›
The script runs entirely in your browser and stores no data externally. It mimics normal human behavior — filling forms and clicking buttons. That said, lottery terms of service prohibit automated entries. Use at your own discretion.
Why does it sometimes ask me to solve a reCAPTCHA? ›
reCAPTCHA requires human interaction and can't be bypassed for free. When detected, the script fills everything else and waits for you to click "I'm not a robot." Many sessions have no captcha at all — in those cases it submits fully automatically.
Broadway Direct shows no ENTER NOW buttons — what happened? ›
A banner appears after a few seconds explaining the reason: all lotteries may be Closed or Upcoming, you may have already entered, or the lottery hasn't opened yet. Check the schedule shown on the show page.
Lucky Seat says "already entered all performances" — what does that mean? ›
All performances matching your time filter (morning/evening) are already entered — they appear in red on the page. Nothing left to do for this show. Check your email for results, or try a different show.
Can I enter the same show twice? ›
On Broadway Direct, duplicate entries for the same performance are disqualified. On Lucky Seat, your latest entry automatically replaces the previous one — re-entering is completely safe.
I won! What do I do next? ›
Check your email immediately — you have about 60 minutes to claim and pay via credit card. On Broadway Direct check your status at lottery.broadwaydirect.com. On Lucky Seat, log into your account dashboard.
Why are TodayTix and Hamilton not supported? ›
TodayTix requires a native mobile app. Hamilton's lottery uses its own platform with strong anti-bot protections. Both are intentionally excluded.
